Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026 · App: Virtual Runner (com.dalcomsoft.virtualrunner)

Virtual Runner ("the App") respects your privacy. The App is an offline application: all data related to your runs is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any external server or shared with third parties.

1. Information We Use

  • Location The App uses your device's GPS location (precise/approximate) to measure your route, distance, pace, and speed during a run. Background location is used so that tracking continues when the App is in the background or the screen is off.
  • Activity Records Run records, statistics, career/progress, and settings are stored in your device's local storage (IndexedDB and app preferences).

2. Storage and Transmission

  • All information is stored only on your device.
  • The App does not transmit any personal data, including location, to any server.
  • There is no sign-up, login, or account.
  • We do not collect advertising identifiers, run analytics, or share data with third parties.

3. Device Permissions

  • Location (fine/coarse/background): measuring run route and distance
  • Notifications: run alerts such as per-kilometer, finish-approaching, and finish (processed on device)
  • Foreground service / Wake lock: keeping location tracking active during a run
  • Vibration: haptic feedback
  • Internet: a baseline permission required for the app to run; the App does not send personal data externally

4. Retention and Deletion

Data remains on your device until you delete it. You can delete your records or all data at any time from Settings > Data in the App. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information targeted at any specific age group; all data is stored solely on the device.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect legal or feature changes. Any changes will be posted on this page.
